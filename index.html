I'm Taylor, a developer & digital designer

I'm a digital designer and developer located in the Bay Area, California with experience in delivering end-to-end marketing campaigns, web development, UX and UI services (and a whole lot of other things).

Most of my professional experience has focused on designing and shipping digital products for a variety of devices and platforms - from responsive web design to email marketing, digital ads and print design. I'm typically focused on the "why" first, then the "how".

Digging into data to help make informed strategic decisions is one of my strong suites, I love finding reasons that explain why people make decisions and what drives their user experience in products I help create. Using that data to inform future designs and decisions is part of my workflow.

I have experience mentoring others and work extremely well in collaborative team settings as well as solo projects.
