Design & Creative Work Overview

I love being a creator. Bold designs, interesting color combinations, balance and visual aestetics always catch my eye.

I have worked professionally as a digital designer for over 10 years. My time in marketing and as a graphic designer has taught me that while I love the process of designing - finding the perfect curve and color palette - design and creative work is something I enjoy much more as a labor of love as opposed to a production team.

Not to say it is something I will never professionally be interested in again, just no what I want to continue solely focus my career around at this time.
